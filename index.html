Formulae & notes

Cockcroft–Gault: CrCl = [(140 − age) × weight × (0.85 if female)] ÷ (72 × SCrmg/dL)

Ideal body weight (Devine): Men 50 + 2.3 × (in − 60) kg; Women 45.5 + 2.3 × (in − 60) kg.

Adjusted body weight: IBW + 0.4 × (ABW − IBW). Commonly applied when actual weight exceeds 120% of ideal.

If actual weight is below ideal, actual weight is generally preferred for dosing.
